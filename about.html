More About The Alarm Team

We are a local team of alarm experts here in the ABQ metro (Albuquerque, Santa Fe, Las Lunas, and Rio Rancho). We follow the consulting agency business model and work with multiple security providers. This allows our team to connect our clients to the best security offers in the industry. Apart from giving you a great deal on monitored security and life safety systems we strive to educate. Whether you work with us or not we want you to know the ins and outs of the system protecting your biggest assets (your home or business). We work with the industry's top providers to create offers designed to help home owners and businesses get the most out of working with us. Our exclusive offers are designed for the market we operate in which in this case is the Albuquerque metro market. Unlike product locked dealerships we have the ability to consult multiple options before signing you up with one. We also help you maximize your insurance (home owners or business) discount when designing your system. Help us help you by booking your free consultation and claiming your free equipment and insurance discount.
