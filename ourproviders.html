Our #1 Provider: Safe Home Direct

We pride ourselves on being security experts and have teamed up with Safe Home Direct to make our Full-Service Security Program (Offer) possible. They have been in business for 25+ years and operate in all 50 states. Safe Home was a dealer for ADT but opened up their own product line under the name Safe Home Direct. All the financing, monitoring, and servicing are comparable to ADT, however, they did this to ditch all the bait and switch ADT offers, complicated corporate processes, upsells, long hold times, and other unnecessary fees. The thing I love most about working with them is what I quote you today is what you get. They also allow us to handle our own tech teams which allows our teams to provide you better service times. This ultimately allows you to get the strength of a solid product designed to actually help customers and the communication of local teams when working with us.
